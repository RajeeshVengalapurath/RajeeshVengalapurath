Introduction

Restaurant Management System is designed to meet the varied requirements of any size or type of restaurants. It provides all the tools a restaurant staff needs for doing their day-to-day jobs including inventory management, restaurant management and recipe management. In addition, it also has supporting features like human resources management, accounts management and system administration.

Features

  • Touch screen or Keyboard/Mouse based interface for order taking and billing on Windows PC or Tablet
  • Multiple restaurant support
  • Stock purchase
  • Inter-godown stock transfer
  • KOT, KOT cancellation
  • KOT printed to printer located at kitchen
  • Billing
  • Billing and Order can be printed on dot matrix or thermal printers
  • Multiple guest billing on single table
  • Takeaway ordering and billing
  • Order table transfer
  • Void bill
  • KOT/ Bill reprint
  • Non-chargeable bills
  • Guest credit and credit repayment
  • Bill payment modes: Cash, Card, Cheque
  • Stock reports
  • Buffet billing and reports

Purchase

  • Purchase & purchase return
  • Purchase order
  • Supplier & supplier payment

Stock

  • Automated stock updation with purchase
  • Opening stock
  • Stock-in & Stock-out
  • Godown
  • Godown transfer
  • Stock reports
  • Stock register

Accounts

  • Ledger
  • Group
  • Bank account
  • Payment & Receipt
  • Ledger report
  • Day book report
  • Sundry debtor & creditor reports
  • Trial balance
  • Other reports
